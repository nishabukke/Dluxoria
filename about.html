About Us

"Turning Houses into Homes, One Design at a Time."

Elevating ordinary homes into spaces that ignite enthusiasm, deliver unparalleled comfort, and evoke happiness is our fundamental passion. Within the realm of interior design, Dluxoria sets itself apart by harnessing creative design ingenuity, embracing sustainability in material selection, and upholding an unyielding dedication to satisfying our clients.

Crafted Solutions by Design Pros

Dluxoria is your unwavering partner in the realm of high-end residential and commercial interior design. Celebrated for our groundbreaking approach, we are specialists in the art of shaping spaces that consistently surpass your expectations. Our unique strength lies in our mastery of space planning, where we custom-tailor each design to align perfectly with your individual requirements, preferences, and financial considerations.

We believe in a collaborative design process, where your active participation is encouraged throughout every project phase. This ensures that your vision and preferences are at the forefront of our creative process. Our team of experienced designers and interior decorators devotes personal attention to each project, guaranteeing that every rupee invested translates into excellent value.
