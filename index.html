Interior Design Firm

Dluxoria, a distinguished luxury home design firm, has been synonymous with creating timeless and sophisticated interior spaces for generations. Located in the vibrant cultural epicenter of Mumbai, we have had the privilege of collaborating with a diverse clientele not only across India but around the globe. Our extensive portfolio spans various industry sectors, encompassing residential, retail, commercial, development, and turnkey contracting projects. Each undertaking is a testament to our unwavering dedication to refined and conscientious design, our considerable technical prowess, and our steadfast commitment to not just meeting but surpassing our clients' expectations.

At Dluxoria, we recognize that the heart of our firm lies in the extraordinary individuals we work with. Consequently, we assemble the most talented luxury interior designers in Mumbai, uniting the industry's most brilliant minds under our banner. Each designer brings a wealth of unique experiences, design sensibilities, and boundless creative flair, all of which we deeply cherish. We nurture these individual talents and weave them together to craft an exclusive brand of interior design and a level of customer service that is unparalleled.

Much like our diverse and dynamic team, our interior design philosophy revolves around the art of harmoniously combining disparate elements to yield a truly exceptional end product. Whether it's the meticulously crafted custom furniture, hand-picked lighting fixtures, the carefully curated color palettes, or the exquisite decor pieces we acquire, we infuse love into every detail. Whether we're undertaking a sleek urban apartment design or embarking on a lavish villa remodel, we invest immense care and attention to bring each facet to life, ultimately creating spaces that radiate synergy and sophistication.
